Welcome to the Pokemon Gym Leaders Directory

Kanto Region

Kanto is the first region introduced in Pokemon. It is home to many famous cities such as Pewter City, Cerulean City, and Viridian City. Trainers travel across Kanto to challenge eight Gym Leaders and earn badges in pursuit of becoming Pokemon Champions. Kanto is known for its diverse environment, ranging from forests and caves to islands and bustling cities.

Johto Region

Johto is located west of Kanto and is connected by land. Unlike Kanto's modern atmosphere, Johto is rich in history, traditions, and ancient legends. Trainers explore historic landmarks such as the Bell Tower and Ruins of Alph while challenging eight Gym Leaders. Johto is famous for introducing many new Pokemon series and expanding the world of Pokemon
